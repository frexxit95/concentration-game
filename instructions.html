How to Play Concentration Game

Welcome to the Concentration Game! Follow these simple instructions to enjoy and enhance your focus:

  1. Click on the "Start New Game" button to begin.
  2. You will see a grid of face-down cards. Each card has a hidden emoji.
  3. Flip two cards at a time by clicking on them.
  4. If the emojis on the flipped cards match, they remain face-up.
  5. If the emojis don't match, the cards flip back face-down.
  6. Your goal is to match all the pairs in the fewest moves possible.
  7. The game ends when all pairs are matched. Your moves will be displayed as the score.
